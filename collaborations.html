Collaborations

Collaboration is an integral part of our research endeavors, and we actively engage with researchers from diverse backgrounds and countries. Over the years, we have established fruitful collaborations with experts in the field, fostering knowledge exchange and advancing scientific discoveries.

Our collaborative network spans over 30 countries, providing a global perspective to our research initiatives. We believe in the power of collective expertise to address complex challenges related to climate change, environmental stress tolerance, and sustainable agriculture.

Notable collaborations include partnerships with institutions and researchers supported by the World Bank, FAO, and other prestigious organizations. These collaborations have enriched our research projects, bringing together different perspectives and methodologies.

We have also recently entered into a collaboration with Acadian Plant Health, a Canadian organization specializing in technologies for plant stress management. This collaboration aims to bridge the gap between research outcomes and practical applications, ensuring that our findings contribute to real-world solutions.

If you are interested in exploring potential collaboration opportunities or have research initiatives aligned with our focus areas, feel free to contact us. We are open to establishing new partnerships that contribute to the advancement of agricultural science and environmental sustainability.
